Aiceberg Mind is much more than a digital educational production company. It's a project of heart and science, born to support parents in their most essential mission: programming their children's inner future.

The Aiceberg Concept

built above. rooted below

Aiceberg Mind embodies the alliance between artificial intelligence and the depth of educational knowledge. Its name symbolizes pedagogy on two levels: the alliance between AI and educational depth.

The visible part: visible, attractive, immersive

What the public sees first:

  • Content that is playful, visually engaging and interactive
  • Generated or enhanced by generative AI
  • Designed to capture children's attention and foster natural and intuitive learning

For a joyful, free, curious, creative, sociable and autonomous child — the visible expression of harmonious development.

The hidden part: invisible but essential

What we don't see... but which constitutes the invisible foundation of each creation. Rigorous research work, nourished by:

  • 📚 The great foundations of child psychology
  • 🧠 Advances in neuroscience and cognitive sciences
  • 🧩 Subconscious programming during the first 7 years of life
  • 🧘‍♀️ Conscious parenting and emotional support techniques
  • 🎯 An integrative vision that connects science, parental intuition and technological innovation

Solid educational foundations programmed from the first years of life as an invisible but fundamental base.

Our Mission

creating tools to shape the invisible

We create multi-format content — videos, audios, eBooks, applications, printables — to support families in building the first years of life, the period when the essential is forged: the unconscious program that will guide tomorrow's adult.

Our Vision

shaping minds from the inside out

🧠 From 0 to 7 years old, everything anchors:
Self-perception, confidence, personal value, inner security, relationship to the world.

That’s why each Aiceberg resource is designed to:

  • Nourish confidence and self-esteem
  • Encourage creativity and autonomy
  • Anchor mental patterns of emotional strength and resilience
  • Offer parents concrete and sensory tools for healthy, joyful development

Yes, we use AI — with consciousness and responsibility

At Aiceberg Mind, we embrace the creative power of Artificial Intelligence — but we do so thoughtfully. We believe that advanced tools come with advanced responsibilities.

🌱 We give back through certified reforestation

We partner with Tree-Nation, Eden Reforestation Projects and Treedom to plant trees, restore ecosystems and create a symbolic "Aiceberg Forest". Every tree matters — and tells a story.

⚡ We support renewable energy and carbon offsetting

Through verified partners like Gold Standard, Atmosfair, and MyClimate, we offset emissions from our digital operations — from server activity to API queries.

💻 We build lean and light

Our platforms follow eco-design principles: green hosting, optimized assets, local fonts and minimal resource use. Less energy, same experience.

🤝 We partner with purpose

We collaborate with educators, scientists and environmental networks to raise awareness around digital ethics and sustainability. Our workshops for kids and families introduce the idea of "conscious AI" — because knowledge shapes action.

📊 Did you know?

Training a large AI model can emit more than 280,000 kg of CO₂ — the equivalent of five lifetime car emissions. That’s why we only use AI when it creates true educational or emotional value.

We don’t believe in digital detox. We believe in digital respect.
